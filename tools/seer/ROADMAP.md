## Professional Gambler Threshold

HMRC classifies you as professional ONLY when ALL:

- Betting is PRIMARY income source
- Sustained profits over multiple years
- Organised systematic approach
- Significant time devoted
- Estimated &lt;500 people in UK meet this

Realistic thresholds to be aware of:

- Under £50,000/year: essentially zero risk
- £50,000–100,000/year: keep good records
- £100,000+/year: get proper tax advice

Seer’s target: grow to £50k+/year while staying clearly recreational in HMRC’s eyes.
